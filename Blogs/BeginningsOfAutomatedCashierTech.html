Beginnings of Automated Cashier Technologies, Inc.

By Vishwa Mudigonda| 5 August | 3 Comments | Startup, B2B Business

A Cornell physics student/researcher and a Computer Science student/Cloud Computing/Data Science intern met after the second year of college to catch up. When they went out for breakfast, they saw long lines and angry customers. The cashiers and the customers at the restaurant were both frustrated with the long wait time to order and these students realized that they had the skills to fix this problem.

The two founded Automated Cashier Tech, LLC in Summer 2017, and began working on a prototype kiosk using the skills they had gained from scientific research and industry internships . Implementing their knowledge and research, the entrepreneurs are in the final stages of development and are now reaching out to people like you to see how they can best customize the product to solve our future clients’ problems.

“Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much” -Helen Keller.

At Automated Cashier Tech, LLC, our team is highly motivated, knowledgeable, ethical and resourceful. Qualified to handle any software or hardware issues, our dedicated staff is committed to helping people with their needs and making successful deals happen. Our team of professionals has the integrity to follow up on our promises, and the expertise to navigate any transaction to ensure you’re fully informed for making the best decision possible.

  • We build Self Service Kiosks with bleeding edge technology to increase the profits of your business and provide customer satisfaction.
  • Ability to make offers for restaurants and create fast, hassle-free transactions, closing in as little as a week.
  • Studies show that people order more food when ordering from a kiosk rather than ordering from a cashier.

*Mission Statement* When a passion for technology and business is integrated with talented individuals who have an uncompromising drive to succeed, amazing things will happen. At Automated Cashier Tech, it’s our goal to not only have a positive effect on ourselves and our families - but also to inspire, motivate, and create lasting change in everyone we encounter. We will treat our clients and team members with respect at all times. Our company will dedicate itself to everlasting education and professional growth that will make our members of the community the leaders of tomorrow. We are the seeds that bloom tomorrow.
